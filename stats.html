Popular Website Emissions

Amazon

  • 1.77B views
  • 71540 kg of carbon dioxide emitted in 2021
  • Carbon emissions up 18% from 2020 and 40% from 2019
  • COVID-19 led to more people shopping online, increasing carbon emissions
  • On average, Amazon produces 39000 kilograms of carbon dioxide for each $1 million of revenue it gets
  • Emissions come not only from deliveries but also from the servers used to run Amazon

Instagram

  • 405000 kg of carbon dioxide emitted annually
  • Posting one photo emits 0.15g of CO2
  • One minute of scrolling Instagram emits 1.5g of CO2
